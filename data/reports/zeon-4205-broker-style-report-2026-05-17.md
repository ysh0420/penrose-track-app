## Investment Conclusion

The stock is no longer just a cheap Japan chemical cyclical. The stronger case is a specialty-materials transition with COP, optical film, battery materials, SWCNT, balance-sheet efficiency, and better shareholder returns. That is enough to stay invested.

The same facts are not enough to chase. Current price is above Koyfin's average target of 2,051.11 JPY, and the analyst distribution is mixed-to-constructive rather than an extreme contrarian setup. The correct broker-style stance is therefore **HOLD: thesis intact, entry unattractive**.

## Bull Case

The bull case is that the market begins to value Zeon less as a generic chemical company and more as a specialty-materials platform. COP capacity expansion, SWCNT battery applications, optical film, capital discipline, and shareholder returns could support a move through the 2,500 JPY target-high level if consensus revisions follow.

## Bear Case

The bear case is that FY2027 expectations fail to rise after the FY2025 beat, COP/SWCNT remain too distant to affect near-term numbers, and the stock gives back the post-results rerating. A break below the 2,050-2,000 zone would weaken the near-term setup; a move toward the 200DMA would require a full thesis review.
